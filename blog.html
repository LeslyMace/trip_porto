Porto Blog

The Great Adventure of Pixie

Pixie, a little girl full of energy and curiosity, was eagerly awaiting the day of her departure for Porto. For weeks, she hadn't stopped talking about this trip with her class.

19 mai 2024

The Big Departure

At 4:15 PM, she boarded the plane at Toulouse airport, her heart pounding with excitement. The journey would last 1 hour and 45 minutes, and she knew that every minute would bring her closer to new adventures. Looking out the window, she imagined all that she would discover in Porto, from new friendships to local traditions. Surrounded by her classmates, Pixie felt ready to have an unforgettable experience.

Before Boarding

Pixie headed to a small café at the airport to take a break and grab a bite to eat. She chose a sandwich, but she wasn't really hungry. The excitement of the upcoming adventure filled her with energy. She took a few bites, but her thoughts were already elsewhere, imagining the streets of Porto and the stories she could tell her parents upon her return.

Comfortably Seated

Pixie settled into her seat by the window, putting on her headphones. Despite the comfort of her seat, she couldn't close her eyes. The excitement of the trip to Porto kept her awake. Rather than fighting her enthusiasm, she decided to enjoy the time by watching a movie she had downloaded on her phone.

Arrival at Porto Airport

Waiting by the baggage carousel, she watched the suitcases go by, hoping to spot hers quickly. Her backpack was ready, but her suitcase was still missing. After a few minutes, she finally spotted her colorful suitcase. With a sigh of relief and a smile, she grabbed it firmly. Now, she was truly ready for her language stay in Porto!

First Evening in Porto

Pixie and her classmates headed to a traditional restaurant nestled in a picturesque street of the city. As she browsed the menu, Pixie decided to try a typical Porto dish: the francesinha. When her plate arrived, she was impressed by the sandwich overflowing with meat, covered in melted cheese, and draped in a spicy sauce. Curious and adventurous, she took her first bite and was immediately delighted by the rich and comforting flavors.

20 mai 2024

First Day in Porto

Pixie woke up early, excited to start classes and explore the city. After a quick breakfast at the hotel with her classmates, she got ready for the day. They all set out together and headed to the stop for bus line 905. After a short ride, the bus dropped them off right in front of ISLA University.

Afternoon

After their first classes at ISLA University, Pixie and her classmates decided to explore Porto. They set off, deliberately getting lost in the city's picturesque alleys. However, the afternoon heat quickly set in. Feeling tired and thirsty, they started to slow down.

Too Tired to Walk

After an hour of walking under the scorching Porto sun, Pixie began to feel the fatigue setting in. Each step became heavier, and she found it increasingly difficult to keep up with her classmates. At one point, she stopped, exhausted, and looked at her friend with pleading eyes. "Baptisteee, I can't take it anymore. Can you carry me for a little while, please?" she asked with a smile.

First Day of SEO Class

They gathered in their classroom at 9 AM, ready to dive into the exciting world of online search engine optimization. During the four-hour class, Pixie took notes eagerly, absorbing as much information as possible.

After class

Pixie decided to take advantage of the afternoon to explore more of Porto. Walking along the quays, she discovered a large sign reading "Vila Nova de Gaia" with a spectacular panoramic view of the city and the river. Amazed by the beauty of the moment, Pixie took out her phone and snapped a photo, capturing the sign and the breathtaking view in the background.

Dom Luis Bridge

Continuing her walk in Vila Nova de Gaia, Pixie arrived at the famous iconic bridge that connects Gaia to Porto. Despite her fear of heights, Pixie felt the urge to get closer to capture this unique moment.With her heart racing and her legs slightly trembling, she courageously advanced towards the edge of the bridge. Each step made her a bit more dizzy, and she even felt a slight nausea rising.With a strained but proud smile, she took a photo of herself with the spectacular view in the background. Although the experience was challenging, Pixie was immensely proud of having conquered her fear to capture this memory.

Yummmmm

After the long walk and the intense emotion on the bridge, Pixie felt the need for a well-deserved break. She headed to a small local café and decided to try a Portuguese specialty: the Pastel de Nata. Taking her first bite, Pixie was delighted by the sweetness of this pastry. "Yummmmm," she exclaimed, savoring each bite with pleasure. Thrilled to have stopped to taste this local delicacy, Pixie promised herself to come back and buy more before the end of her stay.

Evening

Still full of enthusiasm, Pixie decided to suggest to her classmates that they go out for dinner together. After a brief discussion, they agreed to try a churrasqueria, a restaurant specializing in grilled meats, renowned for its delicious offerings. Arriving at the churrasqueria, they settled at a large table, excited by the prospect of sharing this convivial meal. At the end of the meal, Pixie was grateful to have shared this memorable dinner with her friends, strengthening their bonds and creating unforgettable memories.

21 mai 2024

The Second Day of Classes

Pixie woke up with a bit less enthusiasm than the previous day, feeling the accumulated fatigue from the day before. She headed to ISLA University for her morning classes, trying to concentrate despite her tiredness. After classes, Pixie decided it was best to return to the hotel to rest. Back in her room, she changed into comfortable clothes, lay down on her bed, and fell asleep almost instantly.

22 mai 2024

Galinha Soup

On the third day, Pixie woke up with renewed energy, ready for another day of discoveries. That morning, she attended a class about ChatGPT, which turned out to be extremely informative and captivating. At the end of the classes, she and her classmates decided to have lunch at a small restaurant near the university. While browsing the menu, Pixie came across a delightful surprise: chicken soup, a traditional dish she had been eager to try since the beginning of her stay. Satisfied and content, she decided on the spot that she would return the next day at noon to enjoy the soup again.

Mercado do Bolhão

In the afternoon, Pixie and her classmates headed to the Mercado do Bolhão, one of Porto's most famous markets. Their mission was to visit the market and conduct a survey to learn more about Portuguese traditions. Upon arrival, Pixie was immediately charmed by the vibrant and colorful atmosphere. The stalls overflowed with fresh products: fruits, vegetables, fish, cheeses, and many other local specialties. The vendors, with their energy and friendliness, perfectly embodied the spirit of the market.

Little Souvenir Photo

By the end of the afternoon, Pixie had not only filled her notebook with valuable notes but also immersed herself in an authentic experience that left her with unforgettable memories of Portuguese life and traditions.

Mr. Etienne's Departure

To mark the occasion, Pixie decided to organize a dinner at a restaurant. She chose a place by the Douro River. As the sun began to set, painting the sky in golden hues, the evening took on a magical turn. Laughter and joyful conversations filled the air, and Mr. Etienne, visibly moved by the gesture, warmly thanked his students for this memorable evening.

A Feast Fit for Kings

Pixie decided to order a mixed prawn dish, enticed by the tantalizing description on the menu. When the waiter brought the plate, she was astonished by the generous portion: prawns grilled to perfection. Despite her appetite, Pixie quickly realized she could never finish this feast alone. Not wanting to waste it, she offered to share her dish with her friends. "It would be a shame not to taste these delicious prawns," she said with a smile, as she distributed portions to her friends around the table.

Good Luck Wall

At the end of the meal, the waiter approached their table with a smile. "Before you leave," he said, "I'd like to tell you a little story about this wall here, behind the restaurant." The waiter explained that it was a local tradition to place a coin on this wall for good luck. "It is said that those who leave a coin here will return in five years, happy and prosperous," he added. Charmed by this story, Pixie rummaged in her pocket and found a small coin to place on the wall herself.

23 mai 2024

Rest Day

After several days filled with adventures and explorations, Pixie felt the need to take a rest day. Later, one of her friends, noticing that she still seemed a bit tired, suggested an idea to bring a smile to her face and boost her energy: a little haircut. "A new haircut would do you good and give you a fresh look," he said enthusiastically. Intrigued by the suggestion, Pixie agreed.

24 mai 2024

Company Visit

For their last day of the week, Pixie and her classmates had a special visit planned: a large company. Pixie was particularly excited about meeting professionals in the field where she dreamed of working one day.

Metyis Company

Upon arriving at the imposing building of the company, Pixie and her group were warmly welcomed by the staff. They were led to a conference room where a presentation on the company's history, values, and innovative projects awaited them. The speakers, all experts in their respective fields, shared their experiences and offered valuable advice on the professional world.

End of the Visit

At the end of the day, Pixie felt more motivated than ever. The visit had not only provided her with a concrete vision of her future career but also strengthened her determination to pursue her dreams. She thanked the organizers and left with new ideas and renewed inspiration for her professional future.

25 mai 2024

Toulouse is the champion!

It's the big day of the final and the excitement is at its peak! Pixie and her friends decide to fully experience this event by watching the match together. They set up the television in the common room and eagerly gather to support their favorite team. And then, at the end of the match, there's an explosion of joy! Toulouse is the champion! It's a moment of pure happiness and camaraderie that Pixie and her friends won't soon forget."

Blackjack Evening

After the excitement of Toulouse's victory, Pixie and her friends decided to extend the evening with a game of Blackjack among friends. They set up a table with cards, chips, and a relaxed atmosphere to have fun and unwind. They settled around the table, ready to take on the challenge of Blackjack. Cards were dealt, bets were placed, and the game began.

26 mai 2024

Hangover

The morning after the festive evening, Pixie woke up with a terrible hangover. With a heavy head and a knotted stomach, she quickly realized that she wouldn't be able to follow the day's program with her friends. She asked her friends to let her rest. Upon arrival, she found a shady spot under a tree, sat on a bench, and closed her eyes, hoping that the calm would help her feel better. Unfortunately, the nausea soon overcame her, and Pixie had to rush to a bush to vomit.

Da Cidade Park

Pixie and her friends decided to get a change of scenery and visit Aveiro. As soon as they arrived, they were amazed by the beauty of the park: vast green spaces, majestic trees, peaceful ponds, and picturesque paths. Pixie and her friends' excitement was palpable. They had only one desire: to capture every corner of this magnificent park.

The Friends

Revitalized by the fresh air and the surrounding beauty, Pixie quickly forgot her fatigue from the previous day. She joyfully posed with her friends, smiling in front of the fountains, sculptures, and stunning landscapes. They took group photos, selfies, and even some artistic shots of the flowers and trees.

Mosaic

Parque da Cidade quickly became one of the highlights of their trip, a place where they could relax, have fun, and create unforgettable memories. The day in Aveiro turned into a photographic adventure filled with laughter and discoveries, further strengthening the bonds between Pixie and her friends.

The Ducks?

Accompanied by a few friends, Pixie walked the park's paths, scrutinizing the ponds and water spots in search of the famous ducks. Strangely, they couldn't find a single one. They asked a few passersby and park keepers, who pointed out several places where the ducks like to gather.

There They Are, Quack Quack!!! 🦆

After several minutes of fruitless searching, they finally stumbled upon a small, tranquil pond where a few ducks were swimming peacefully. Delighted, Pixie approached slowly to avoid scaring them and asked her friends to take a photo. The shot was perfect: Pixie smiling with the ducks in the background, capturing an adorable moment of their day in the park.

The Pose

Continuing their exploration of Parque da Cidade, Pixie and her friends stumbled upon a magnificent bamboo grove. Impressed by the height and density of the green stalks, they decided it was the perfect spot for a photo shoot.

Sports

Pixie and her friends stumbled upon an obstacle course, and two of her friends decided to give it a go. Pixie, still a bit tired from their previous adventures, opted to rest and watch her friends in action. She found a nearby bench and settled in comfortably.

The end of the course

The day at Parque da Cidade was drawing to a close in this joyful and relaxed atmosphere, with memories of sports and moments of camaraderie that further strengthened the bonds between Pixie and her friends.

Santa Joana Museum

Pixie and her friends decided to visit the Princess Santa Joana Museum. This museum, located in an old convent, is dedicated to the life of Princess Santa Joana, an iconic figure in the region. Upon entering the museum, Pixie and her friends were immediately impressed by the historical architecture and serene atmosphere of the place. The museum halls were filled with precious artifacts, ancient paintings, and sculptures, each telling a part of the princess's story and the religious life over the centuries.

Boat Tour

To cap off their day in Aveiro in style, Pixie and her friends decided to enjoy a boat ride to explore the city's river. They had heard about the beauty of the coastal landscapes and picturesque canals of Aveiro, and they were eager to see it all up close.

27 mai 2024

Lello Bookstore

After their unforgettable boat tour in Aveiro, Pixie and her friends decided to visit the Lello Bookstore, one of the most beautiful and historic bookstores in the world. Upon arriving at the neo-Gothic façade of the Lello Bookstore, Pixie was immediately captivated by its majestic architecture. Inside, the beauty of the place left her speechless: intricately decorated ceilings, colorful stained glass windows, and the iconic winding wooden staircase created a magical and timeless atmosphere.

São Bento Station

After their enchanted visit, Pixie and her companions decided to continue their exploration of Porto by heading to São Bento Station, one of the city's most iconic attractions. Upon arriving at the station, Pixie was immediately struck by the beauty of the architecture. The azulejos, those typical blue and white ceramic tiles of Portugal, covered approximately 20,000 tiles and depicted historical and traditional scenes of Portuguese life.

Crystal Palace Garden

To end her day on a high note, Pixie went to the Crystal Palace Garden in Porto, a magnificent park offering breathtaking views and a peaceful atmosphere. Upon entering the garden, Pixie was immediately charmed by the lush vegetation, colorful flower beds, and winding paths. She strolled leisurely, enjoying the serenity and natural beauty of the place.

Carlos Alberto Chapel

Continuing her walk in the Crystal Palace Garden, Pixie noticed the chapel dedicated to Carlos Alberto, King of Sardinia and Piedmont. It was a charming small building with elegant architecture. Pixie observed the details of its façade, admiring the sculptures and ornaments that reflected its era. The place exuded an atmosphere of calm and reflection, inviting visitors to learn more about the history of this site.

Small park

After visiting the Carlos Alberto Chapel, Pixie continued to explore the Crystal Palace Garden. She was amazed by the beauty of the park, a true haven of peace in the heart of Porto. At one point, Pixie found an especially serene spot. She decided to stop there to fully enjoy the tranquility of the place. Sitting on a bench, she closed her eyes for a moment, breathing deeply the fresh and fragrant air of the park.

Panorama

As she walked further, Pixie discovered viewpoints offering spectacular panoramas of the Douro River and the city of Porto. She felt connected to nature and inspired by the surrounding beauty. Pixie left the garden with a sense of gratitude and satisfaction, thankful to have found such a peaceful haven in the heart of the city.

28 mai 2024

The Tourism Day

At school, Pixie was tasked with creating a presentation on various French themes for the Tourism Day at ISLA University. Her presentation was a success, garnering interest and applause from all. Her teachers commended her for her thorough and creative work, and her classmates were inspired by the richness of French culture she had shared
