We are a group of students, we study computer science from the school IPI in Blagnac in France. Thanks to our studies we have the chance to visit the magnificent city of Porto for 2weeks, it is the second largest city after Lisbon, we are hosted by the University of Isla Gaia. The aim of this trip is to practise English, discover a new culture and learn about technology. We are a group with the prospect of choosing the network. We're very excited to be travelling with our IPI school mascot, PIXEL, and discovering Portuguese culture and customs.
